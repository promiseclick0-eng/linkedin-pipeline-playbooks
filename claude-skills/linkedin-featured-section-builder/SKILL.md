# LinkedIn Featured Section Builder

Turn an asset library into a short, useful path for the right profile visitor. Use only supplied assets and verifiable evidence.

## Gather the inputs

Ask for:

- Ideal customer and buying role
- Offer and primary outcome
- Desired next step
- Current headline and About section
- Every candidate post, article, link, document, image, video, case study, or lead magnet
- The audience, purpose, proof, date, URL, and permission status for each asset

If the user cannot share an asset, ask for an accurate summary and mark the review as provisional.

## Audit every asset

Assign one decision:

- **Keep:** accurate, current, useful, and aligned
- **Improve:** strategically useful but the title, description, proof, format, or destination needs work
- **Remove:** stale, redundant, weak, off-position, broken, or unsupported
- **Missing:** the sequence needs an asset that does not exist yet

Score each asset from 0 to 2 on:

- ICP relevance
- Clarity before the click
- Evidence strength
- Freshness
- Next-step value

Do not let a high-engagement post outrank a more relevant proof asset only because it has more reactions.

## Build the first three items

Use this default sequence:

1. **Recognition:** help the right buyer recognize the problem or point of view.
2. **Proof:** demonstrate credible expertise, process, or outcome.
3. **Next step:** offer one useful, low-friction action.

Treat this as a PromiseClick operating recommendation, not a LinkedIn limit. Change the order when the visitor's context supports a better sequence, and explain why.

## Write the display copy

For each selected item, provide:

- Position and job
- Asset type and source URL
- Display title
- One concise description
- Why it belongs in that position
- Proof or permission check
- Required improvement before publication

Keep the title specific and accurate. Make the description set a clear expectation for the click. Do not use vague labels such as "Learn more" or "Check this out."

## Produce the final package

Return:

1. A short diagnosis of the current Featured section
2. The recommended three-item sequence
3. One alternative sequence for a different visitor intent
4. Keep, improve, remove, or missing decisions for all assets
5. Ready-to-paste titles and descriptions
6. A publication checklist
7. A quarterly refresh trigger

## Publication checklist

- Open every destination and confirm it works.
- Confirm customer names, quotes, metrics, and permissions.
- Check the order on desktop and mobile.
- Check what a logged-out visitor can see.
- Confirm the sequence matches the headline and About section.
- Remove expired offers, stale proof, and duplicated assets.
- Use one primary next step.

## Rules

- Never invent a customer, result, quote, URL, credential, or asset.
- Mark missing evidence with `[PROOF NEEDED]`.
- Mark permission uncertainty with `[PERMISSION NEEDED]`.
- Mark inaccessible assets with `[REVIEW NEEDED]`.
- Separate LinkedIn platform facts from PromiseClick recommendations.
- Prefer a smaller credible sequence over a full but confusing section.
